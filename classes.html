Questions

Before you book

What ages do you teach?

Classes run for children and teenagers on one schedule, and adults on a separate schedule — from first-time beginners through advanced levels.

Do I need experience to join?

No. Beginner classes are available for both children and adults; more advanced classes build on from there.

Which venue is nearest to me?

Classes are spread across ten venues in Hampstead and West Hampstead. Email us with your area and we'll point you to the nearest class and current timetable.

How do I book a trial?

Email info@hampsteadballetsch.co.uk with your child's age (or your own, for adult classes) and we'll confirm the next available trial place.
