Classical ballet · children & adults

Proper training, close to home, since 1996.

Hampstead Ballet School has taught classical ballet across Hampstead and West Hampstead for almost thirty years, under teachers with professional stage careers of their own. Our students have gone on to some of the world's leading ballet schools.
